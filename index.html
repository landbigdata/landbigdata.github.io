what we do

This project "Alternative Trajectories for Sustainable Settlements Development" is being conducted at the Institute for Environmental Studies (IVM) of VU University Amsterdam, with finincial supports primarily from China Scholarship Council(CSC). The objective is to improve the representation of urban land in land-use models, in order to assess future urban land change trajectories and their consequential impacts.
